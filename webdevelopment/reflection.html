Reflection

As I begin wrapping up my last term here at the University of Missouri I now am sitting at the crossroads of what decision I should make in continuing my career...

I’ve admittedly been very tunnel visioned and have purely focused on graduating, especially with a very tough very cramped last couple of semesters. So the next step does feel a bit daunting, that’s why I think this resume site project can potentially be very helpful.

The job that makes up the bulk of my work experience is being a Sales Associate at Home Depot. I’ve spent a significant amount of time working at Home Depot, even though it’s not directly tied to my field, I grew a lot working in one place for so long. I never moved into a higher position due to my inability to commit while focusing on school, but I was able to become a trusted member of the team. I was entrusted with the training of new employees and left to run the department. So while my resume may initially look a little thin due to the small number of positions, I am a determined worker and hopefully I can make that shine through.

I did for a few months work as an IT intern at the college I previously attended, Three Rivers College. This is more directly tied to my field and I directly worked with the Technology and Computer Service departments on the campus and helped with general day to day tasks. While I wasn’t there for the longest time, it proved that I am capable and competent in this field and that I have the knowledge to be successful in it.

Where I need to look next is gaining more relevant experience in the field of IT. As mentioned before I don’t have an immediate next step planned out but taking a look at my work experience helps me reflect on what I’m good at, where I’ve been successful, and where I should look to improve. While it’s not my favorite line of work, I’ve been very successful and appreciated in customer service and have a strength in working with other people.

Karson Null, 07/14/2026
